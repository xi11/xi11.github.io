About

I am a Principal Data Scientist in the Department of Translational Molecular Pathology and the Institute for Data Science in Oncology at The University of Texas MD Anderson Cancer Center. I develop AI for histopathology and integrate it with spatial transcriptomics and other omics to study tumor ecosystems and treatment response.
