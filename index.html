Biography

I was a post-doctoral researcher at Institut Fresnel and Aix-Marseille University from Nov. 2019 to Dec. 2019. Before that, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Ecole Centrale de Marseille (ECM) and Institut Fresnel, where I was supervised by Prof. Mouloud Adel and Prof. Caroline Fossati. I received my M.E. and B.E. degrees from Beihang University, Beijing and Northwestern Polytechnical University, Xi'an, respectively.

My research interests include machine learning and deep learning, especially applications to medical image analysis.
