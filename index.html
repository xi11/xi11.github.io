About

I am currently working as a principal data scientist in the lab led by Dr. Yinyin Yuan at The University of Texas MD Anderson Cancer Center (2023 to present). Prior to that, I was a postdoc in Dr. Yuan’s lab at the Institute of Cancer Research (ICR), UK (2020 to 2023), and received my Ph.D. in Optics, Photonics and Image Processing at Ecole Centrale Marseille, France (2016 to 2019).
